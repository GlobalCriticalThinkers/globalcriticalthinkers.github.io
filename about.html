About Global Critical Thinkers

Developing better thinkers for a more thoughtful world.

Global Critical Thinkers is an educational competition platform designed to develop critical thinking, evidence-based decision-making, communication, and responsible leadership through real-world challenges.

Why GCT exists

A world with more information, and a growing need for better judgment.

Today's world offers more information than any generation before it has had access to. What has not grown at the same pace is the ability to evaluate that information carefully — to separate a strong argument from a persuasive one, and a sound decision from a fast one.

Global Critical Thinkers was established to give young people a structured place to practice exactly that: reasoning through real, unresolved problems, under real scrutiny, without a script to fall back on.

Our vision

A generation that reasons carefully before it decides.

Our long-term vision is an educational one: to help develop a generation of thinkers who are more thoughtful in how they reason, stronger in how they communicate, and more responsible in how they decide.

This vision extends beyond any single competition. It is about the habits of mind that stay with a participant long after the day of the event has ended.

Our philosophy

Five principles that guide how we think.

Critical Thinking

Every problem deserves to be questioned before it is answered.

Evidence Before Opinion

A position is only as strong as the evidence that supports it.

Respectful Dialogue

Ideas can be challenged rigorously while people are treated with respect.

Better Decisions

The goal is not to win an argument, but to reach the strongest possible decision.

Continuous Growth

Thinking is a skill that develops with practice, feedback, and reflection.

How we design competitions

Consistent educational principles behind every edition.

Every Global Critical Thinkers competition, regardless of theme or venue, is built on the same underlying educational structure.

Real-World Challenges

Problems drawn from authentic situations, not textbook scenarios.

Collaborative Discussion

Structured space for reasoning to be tested before it is presented.

Structured Presentations

A clear format for communicating reasoning, not performing it.

Standardized Evaluation

The same framework applied consistently to every participant.

Personalized Reports

Individual feedback that outlasts the day of the competition.

A full breakdown of the competition format is available on the Competition Overview and How You're Evaluated sections of the main page.

Beyond competition

Learning does not end when the competition does.

A single competition is a starting point, not a destination. Future editions aim to strengthen the community around Global Critical Thinkers, creating ongoing opportunities for participants to keep learning, keep contributing, and take on greater responsibility over time.

The long-term direction of this community is outlined on the Building an Institution section of the main page.

Our commitment

What we hold ourselves to.

Fairness

The same standards, applied consistently to every participant.

Transparency

Clear communication about how the competition works and how it is judged.

Educational Quality

Every element is designed to teach, not only to test.

Respect

For every participant, judge, assessor, and facilitator involved.

Continuous Improvement

Each edition is an opportunity to refine and strengthen what came before it.
