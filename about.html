About Global Critical Thinkers

Developing better thinkers for a more thoughtful world.

Global Critical Thinkers is an educational competition platform designed to develop critical thinking, evidence-based decision-making, communication, and responsible leadership through real-world challenges.

Why we exist

More information than ever. Not, on its own, better judgment.

No generation before this one has had so much information within reach. What has not kept pace is the ability to weigh it well — to tell a strong argument from a merely persuasive one, and a sound decision from a fast one.

Global Critical Thinkers was founded on that gap. We wanted to give young people a place to practice reasoning through real, unresolved problems, under genuine scrutiny, with no script to hide behind.

Our aim reaches beyond any single competition. We hope to help shape a generation that reasons more carefully, communicates more responsibly, and carries better judgment into every decision that follows.

Our philosophy

Five principles that guide how we think.

We start from a simple premise: sound thinking is not an inborn gift but a discipline, one that sharpens with practice, feedback, and honest reflection. Everything we build rests on five ideas.

Critical Thinking

Every problem deserves to be questioned before it is answered.

Evidence Before Opinion

A position is only as strong as the evidence that supports it.

Respectful Dialogue

Ideas can be challenged rigorously while people are treated with respect.

Better Decisions

The goal is not to win an argument, but to reach the strongest possible decision.

Continuous Growth

Thinking is a skill that develops with practice, feedback, and reflection.

What makes GCT different

Most competitions reward confidence.
GCT rewards clarity.

Most competitions crown whoever argues loudest. GCT was built around a different question: who actually reasoned their way to the right answer, and can they show their work? That difference changes everything about how you'll spend your day here.

Real Judgment, Not Rehearsal

Most competitions reward a well-rehearsed pitch. GCT gives you a real-world challenge you've never seen, and asks what your judgment looks like without a script to hide behind.

Scrutiny, Not Applause

Most competitions want to be impressed. Our judges want to be convinced. They will find the weakest point in your thinking and press on it until it either holds or doesn't.

A Discipline, Not a Day

Pre-event workshops in Critical Thinking and Communicating Ideas, an online technical meeting (gladi resik), and a full day of reasoning under real conditions — built to leave you thinking differently, not just occupied for a day.

A Path, Not an Ending

Most competitions end when the trophy is handed out. Strong performers here earn priority consideration for an exclusive champions-only competition planned for late 2027.

The disciplines

Analysis

The discipline of seeing past the obvious framing. You learn to ask what's actually being asked, before reaching for an answer.

02

Evidence

The discipline of intellectual honesty. You learn to distrust your first instinct until the data has earned your confidence.

03

The discipline of deciding under uncertainty. You learn to commit to a position you can actually defend, not just one that sounds good.

Our standards

Code of Integrity

Excellence is measured not only by achievement, but by the integrity behind every decision.

The quality of a competition depends not only on how participants perform, but also on the principles everyone chooses to uphold.

Equal Opportunity

Every participant competes under the same standards regardless of background, school, institution, or previous experience.

Independent Judging

Every evaluation is based solely on the quality of reasoning, evidence, and decision-making.

Academic Integrity

Participants are expected to produce original work, respect intellectual honesty, and never fabricate evidence or plagiarize ideas.

Respectful Dialogue

Ideas should be challenged rigorously. People should always be treated respectfully.

Evidence-Based Reasoning

Strong conclusions require strong evidence. Arguments should be supported by logic rather than assumptions.

Integrity is not a separate part of this competition.

It is the standard expected from every participant, judge, assessor, facilitator, and organizer.

How we design competitions

Consistent educational principles behind every edition.

Every Global Critical Thinkers competition, regardless of theme or venue, is built on the same underlying educational structure — one designed to reward how well a participant reasons, not how much they've memorized.

A full breakdown of the competition format is available on the Competition Overview and How You're Evaluated sections of the Competition page.

Building an institution

Learning does not end when the competition does.

A single competition is a starting point, not a destination. Future editions aim to strengthen the community around Global Critical Thinkers, creating ongoing opportunities for participants to keep learning, keep contributing, and take on greater responsibility over time.

One framework. Endless challenges. Every edition follows the same competition methodology — the same rigor, the same standard of judgment — while introducing a completely new case, venue, and industry focus.
