The mission

A real case.
A real deadline.

No hypotheticals, no rehearsed pitches. Every team receives the same live case, sourced from an actual Indonesian business, and works against the same clock. What separates the winners isn't confidence. It's how they think under pressure.

The analysis

Where the thinking
gets rigorous.

01

Analyst

Breaks the case down to its real problem, not the one sitting on the surface.

02

Financial Analyst

Tests every assumption against the numbers. If it doesn't hold up, it doesn't go forward.

03

Strategist

Turns the analysis into a plan the team can actually stand behind.

04

Presenter

Everything the team built comes down to this. Clear thinking, spoken plainly.

The presentation

The idea meets the room.

  • 10 minutes to present the full recommendation, unaided by a script.
  • Any team member can be asked to answer, not only the Presenter.
  • Clarity over polish. Judges are trained to reward substance, not delivery flourish.

The defense

Every idea gets challenged.
Every ego gets left at the door.

The judges push back, hard. The team holds its ground or changes its mind in real time. Both are wins, if the reasoning behind them is sound.

15-minute open cross-examination
Judged on reasoning, not just conclusions
