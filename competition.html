Competition Overview

One challenge. One day. One standard of judgment.

Every participant works through the same real-world challenge, under the same conditions, using the same evaluation framework — first on their own, then as part of a team — regardless of background, school, or prior experience.

Assessment

How You're Evaluated

Great decisions begin with great reasoning.

Every participant is assessed at each stage of the competition — individual and collaborative — using one standardized evaluation framework designed to measure not only outcomes, but also the quality of thinking behind every decision.

01

Critical Analysis

Ability to identify the real problem instead of reacting to the obvious one.

02

Evidence Evaluation

Ability to distinguish facts, assumptions, opinions, and weak arguments.

03

Decision Making

Ability to choose the strongest solution under uncertainty.

04

Communication

Ability to explain complex reasoning clearly and logically.

05

Reflection

Ability to defend, reconsider, and improve ideas when challenged.

Assessment Philosophy

We do not reward the fastest answer.
We reward the strongest reasoning.

45 Minutes to form your own position before anyone else's opinion enters the room

Communicating ideas

A clear mind sounds like one.

  • 10 minutes to make your reasoning legible — not to perform it, just to make it followable.
  • Anyone can be questioned, not only whoever is speaking. Every idea in the room has to be defensible by the person who holds it.
  • Clarity outranks polish. Judges are trained to listen for sound thinking, not confident delivery.

The examination

This is where thinking
either holds — or it doesn't.

Judges will find the weakest point in your reasoning and press on it. You can hold your position or change your mind — both are respected, as long as either one is earned through logic, not stubbornness or nerves.

15 minutes of direct, unscripted questioning
Judged on how you think, not how you sound

Partners

Built together with trusted partners.

Global Critical Thinkers collaborates with universities, schools, and industry organizations that share our standard for how thinking should be tested. These institutional and educational partners help shape a competition experience grounded in real academic and professional rigor.

Strategic partners contribute expertise, resources, and platforms that extend the competition's reach — from evaluation design to opportunities for participants beyond the competition day itself.

Every partnership is chosen deliberately. It's how GCT stays connected to real institutions and real standards, rather than operating in isolation. Partnership opportunities are currently open, and official partners will be named here as they're confirmed.
