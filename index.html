The mindset

Certainty is easy.
Clear thinking isn't.

GCT is not designed to see who can perform confidence. It's designed to see how you actually think when the answer isn't obvious, the clock is running, and no one is going to agree with you by default. What separates strong thinkers isn't how sure they sound. It's how carefully they've reasoned before they speak.

Registration

Claim your place
at the table.

Global Critical Thinkers is an individual competition built around collaborative problem-solving. You register as an individual, and throughout the competition, your reasoning, communication, and judgment remain at the heart of the assessment. Along the way, the organizing committee places you into a diverse discussion team, where your ideas are challenged, your reasoning refined, and your arguments sharpened alongside others. Outstanding teamwork is recognized through the Best Team award. No hypotheticals. No safety net — and no one else's confidence to hide behind.

  • One real-world challenge based on an authentic situation
  • Pre-event workshops in Critical Thinking and Communicating Ideas, plus direct feedback from an experienced judging panel
  • An official certificate and a personal performance report card from assessors
